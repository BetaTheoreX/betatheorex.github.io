How TheoreX Works

Learn to succeed

TheoreX believes in tangible value. What good is a degree that does not help you land a job? Education is an investment, and every investment is defined by its ROI: Return on Investment. Maximise your potential by taking a course from TheoreX: certified courses from the very best of institutes with none of the baggage. Study at your convenience and get certified.

Practice makes everything perfect

From A to Z, we at TheoreX cover every base of your education. Want a job in Web Development? Take our course. Complete with examples and practice sets, you are subjected to the same academic rigour as a conventional “indoors” course at any top level university. Take our professionally developed assignments and have your work critiqued by the best professionals.

Show off your smarts

TheoreX is a platform for building your professional portfolio. Showcase your past work and current projects to prospective employers and peers. You can ask for constructive criticism, even new work if you are interested. With TheoreX, you can dive right in to the work you want to be doing!

Connect with professionals

TheoreX is a community that encourages information exchange and believes in holistic growth. Participate in discussions and talk about whatever suits your fancy in “Open TheoreX Forum”. Engage like-minded individuals and professionals. Learn about what’s happening in your surroundings, in other industries. Network and Grow!



Get hired

Interested in a job? TheoreX is a platform that works both ways: helping people get educated by nurturing their skillsets and companies in finding great employees. With a TheoreX certified course under your belt, companies are aware that you have what it takes. Showcase your past projects so that they know what you are capable of. Get Noticed; Get Hired!
