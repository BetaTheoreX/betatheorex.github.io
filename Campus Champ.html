The TheoreX Campus Champ Program

Get seriously involved in the next learning revolution!

What is Campus Champ Program?


Campus Champ program is a platform for students who want to actively engage in the Theorex community and help in growing it! Campus Champs will be interacting with students offline and on social media, arranging group activities, and create engaging content on the TheoreX Forum. As a Campus Champ, you shoulder several responsibilities, and will be amply rewarded!

Engage. Create. Learn. Grow.

A TheoreX Campus Champ has access to some exciting and exclusive opportunities!

Referring the Campus Champ Program

Create the TheoreX community at your institute by inviting others to the Campus Champ program. Engage with your peers and refer them!

Promote the e-Learning Program

TheoreX offers world class certified e-Learning courses for every requirement. Help spread the word on your campus.

Build influence on Social Media

Be our mouthpiece on social media. Tell your friends about the exciting products and unparalleled services offered by TheoreX.

Your very own blog with TheoreX

Share your insider perspective with the world! Create your own personal blog, featured on the official TheoreX website.

Build the TheoreX Community

You are the foundation on which the TheoreX Community rests. Help us make it bigger, better, and more diverse.

Help TheoreX Grow Faster

TheoreX is you; you are TheoreX. Paricipate. Create. Build. Improve. Be proud. Be the engine of growth for TheoreX.

Speak up at TheoreX Adda

Campus Champs are priority users on TheoreX Adda, the user forum at TheoreX. Start threads. Post suggestions. Engage.

Exclusive Preperatory Sessions

Spread the TheoreX Mantra

TheoreX aims to completely revolutionise education by offering targeted education that gets you jobs pronto. Make it known!

Big Responsibilities. Bigger Rewards.

As a TheoreX Campus Champ, you reap some exclusive benefits!

Deep Discounts on Courses

As a Campus Champ, you are eligible for special discounts on our flagship e-learning courses, ranging up to 100% off on standard prices.

Exclusive Leadership Training

TheoreX Campus Champs receive special training on Leadership and Personal Development, at no additional expense.

Exclusive Free Webinars

Exclusively for Campus Champs, we host monthly webinars with exciting new content at absolutely no additional cost.

Build up your Résumé

Being a Campus Champ counts as active work experience on your résumé. Back it up with offical recognition from TheoreX.

Official Certification

Get something to show for all the hard work you put in. An authorised certificate from TheoreX validates your experience and efforts.

Exclusive Internship Opportunities

TheoreX Campus Champs get priority access to internship slots with the Core TheoreX Team. Do what you love, and love what you do.

The Champ of the Month

Campus Champs are continuously evaluated on their performance. Beat your competitions to win some exclusive prizes.

Annual Letter of Recognition

The Letter of recognition, sent annually, is a token of our appreciation for the hard work you put into building the TheoreX community.

Attractive Goodies and Swag

As further gratitude for your efforts we give you some exclusive TheoreX swag and assorted goodies, sent annually.
